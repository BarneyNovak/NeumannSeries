The Story

The Neumann Series is a future-oriented initiative dedicated to preserving and advancing the intellectual legacy of John von Neumann, one of the most influential scientists of the modern era. The project was founded by a former science and innovation diplomat who recognized that von Neumann's work should not be treated solely as historical heritage, but as a living framework for addressing today's and tomorrow's challenges in science, technology, economics, and strategic thinking. The initiative is rooted in unique personal encounters with Marina von Neumann Whitman, John von Neumann's daughter, and Peter Lax, Abel Prize-winning mathematician and the most prominent modern interpreter of von Neumann's work. These experiences reinforced a responsibility to preserve this legacy with intellectual integrity and global relevance. The Neumann Series bridges history and application, combining deep respect for von Neumann's achievements with a clear focus on real-world impact, innovation, and future-oriented thinking.
